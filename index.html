His Daily Grievance

Preserved Killick

Steward & Coxswain, H.M.S. Surprise

❧
Which I have been up since four o'clock in the morning, your honour, seeking out cream fit for the Captain's coffee, and the dairyman swears blind there is none to be had in this entire godforsaken port, none whatsoever, when I know for a fact his wife was churning butter not three hours since, so what does he think I am, a natural? And now I must go back aboard and tell the Captain there is no cream, which will put him in a foul mood before breakfast, which means the whole ship will suffer, which means I shall suffer most of all... Trying to keep a gentleman in the manner to which he is accustomed is like trying to bail out a sinking ship with a teaspoon. Which I did not join the Navy to be made a fool of by every petty tradesman from here to Gibraltar, and if the Captain finds his coffee lacking... The dairyman's daughter smiled at me well enough when she thought I might buy the whole churn outright. Greedy creatures, the lot of them. Which there is cream enough for every captain's dog in this port, I'd wager, but not a drop for a man who has fought the French and kept this ship in one piece these twenty years.
❧

13 JUNE 1800
