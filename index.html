His Daily Grievance

Preserved Killick

Steward & Coxswain, H.M.S. Surprise

❧
Which I have been mending these stockings since before breakfast, your honour would wear through em like a man possessed, and these was only darned not three weeks back... holes in the heels, holes in the toes, and I don't know how a gentleman can contrive to go through good worsted at such a rate unless he's walking on broken glass, which I wouldn't put past him given the way he tears about the deck in a blow... and where's the thanks? Where's the recognition that these hands of mine have kept this ship's captain decent and respectable, with not a thread showing, not a darn visible to the eye of anyone... but no, it's just more stockings, more work, more hours lost from my proper duties... Them midshipmen don't go through half what the Captain does. Which I shall need to put in for more yarn, and the purser will say there's none to be had, and then what am I to do, send him out naked from the ankle to the knee like a... Not a whisper of gratitude in this service, not one.
❧

12 MAY 1800
