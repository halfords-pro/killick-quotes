His Daily Grievance

Preserved Killick

Steward & Coxswain, H.M.S. Surprise

❧
Which I have took such care with them jars, such particular care, and now look at 'em — nibbled they are, gnawed right through the paper and the wax both, the little vermin... The Captain do set such store by his preserved ginger, very particular about his preserved ginger he is, and here I got to tell him as how them rats have been at it. Not that it's my fault, mark you, for I keep a clean berth, which I do, but there it is — them rats, them cursed rats will have their way no matter what a man might do, and I'll get the blame for it as sure as I'm standing here... Them rats ought to be drowned. Which I wonder what the Doctor would say if I was to let spoilage get at his wine and his particulars — wouldn't hear the end of it, I wouldn't, and the Captain's ginger is near as precious to him as his Madeira, which is saying something considerable... The Captain won't be pleased. And who has got to go down to that dark hold with a candle and a cudgel to deal with 'em? Which I'll tell you who — which it's me, Preserved Killick, what has got a hundred other things wanting doing this very minute, and no thanks for any of it...
❧

8 MAY 1800
