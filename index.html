His Daily Grievance

Preserved Killick

Steward & Coxswain, H.M.S. Surprise

❧
Which I have asked at three separate establishments, your honour, three, and not one of 'em can provide a decent cream — and the Captain's coffee sitting there going cold whilst I'm out trudging about in this heat, and him expecting it at the proper temperature same as he always does, and what am I to tell him, that the whole town's gone short, that there's not a drop of decent cream to be had from here to... Them skim-milk merchants wouldn't know proper cream if it rose up and slapped 'em on the cheek. Which the Doctor could probably make something answer with one of his powders, but that ain't the point, sir, that ain't the point at all — the Captain don't want no powder in his coffee, he wants cream, proper cream, fresh cream, and here I am with nothing but... Tell me true — has this port ever had anything worth the having? Which I don't know what the world's coming to when a steward can't source the simplest provision, simple as that, and I've been at this since before dawn with not so much as a mug of weak tea myself, and Mr Pullings watching me like I'm skiving when anyone with eyes can see I'm doing the work of two men... Can't get cream. Can get weevils by the pint, but cream? That's asking too much,
❧

14 APRIL 1800
